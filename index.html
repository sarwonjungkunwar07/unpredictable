About me

Mr. Sarwon jung kunwar AkA Marshall

Born and raised in Nepal, kailali, My first programming language is c and c++ then i learn java for android app Development. And i got my start designing and coding websites for a local agency. After 1/2 years and 100+ shipped sites, I started a mobile design + dev shop where we experimented with some silly ideas, and some more practical.


Then in 2020, a little startup out of CP found us and trusted us enough to learn how to build an Android app and web-site on the job. During the more than 1 years spent there, I transitioned into a leader on the product design team—helping to ship new products, a workplace platform and build a team of incredible designers.


Now in 2023, I’m excited to be taking on a new challenge with an amazing Canadian based company to create important new products to help entrepreneurs build successful businesses.
